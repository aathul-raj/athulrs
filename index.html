about me.


Hi! I'm Athul Suresh. I'm currently an Applied Mathematics (Comp Sci Emphasis) major at Texas A&M, with a Comp Sci minor. In my free time I code cool stuff, develop and manage websites, rock climb, and play the guitar.

I am excited to continue my studies and pursue a career as a software developer, where I can use my skills and dedication to create impactful products with cool people.



projects.

2. Movie Rec Algorithm - An algorithm I made that utilizes cosine similarity and the Movielens dataset to reccommend 10 similar movies to a single one that you input. Built with Python and Scikit.

4. Chorus - A music rec site. Enter three albums or three artists and Chorus will recommend you three more! Built with Node.JS, React, and OpenAI.

work.


Back in high school, I was the founder and editor-in-chief of the student-led news site, Travis Tribune. This experience helped me hone my skills in creating and managing online platforms. My software internship at RetailMeNot significantly enhanced my technical capabilities, where I was responsible for developing an internal API and crafting a Python script to automate error handling processes. I'm also the website maintainer for the Quantum Computing site Quantum Curious.

I've earned recognition for my talent, including winning the 2021 FBLA Website Design competition in Texas (finishing as an international semi-finalist) and placing 2nd in the 2022 FBLA Digital Video Production competition. Collectively, these experiences have fortified my expertise in website development, content management, and software engineering.
